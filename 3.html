실크 소닉(Silk Sonic)은 미국의 R&B 슈퍼 듀오으로, 가수 브루노 마스와 가수 앤더슨 팩이 멤버로 있다. 2021년 3월 5일, 데뷔 싱글 "Leave the Door Open"을 발매했다.


In 2017, Anderson .Paak was the opening act for Bruno Mars on the European leg of the 24K Magic World Tour.[1] In April, Mars and Paak worked together at Abbey Road Studios in London with Nile Rodgers and Guy Lawrence for Chic's album It's About Time, and worked on music while on tour that appeared on Paak's album Ventura.[2][3] On February 26, 2021, Mars announced the band saying, "[Silk Sonic] locked in and made an album."[4] In a press release, they said the band was named by Bootsy Collins after he heard the album.[5] "Leave the Door Open", the lead single for their upcoming album, was released on March 5, along with the intro song, hosted by Collins.[6] The band made their television debut at the 63rd Annual Grammy Awards performing the single, alongside a tribute to Little Richard consisting of "Long Tall Sally" and "Good Golly, Miss Molly".
